Lessons & Approach

Who I teach

Children aged 4+, preparing for exams, adult beginners/returners, and advanced students (including Diploma level).

How lessons work

  • Fundamentals: relaxed posture, sound production, technique (scales/arpeggios/etudes)
  • Musical understanding: style, phrasing, harmony; duet playing to build listening
  • Performance: mock exams, concerts, confidence and stagecraft

Exam prep

ABRSM/Trinity grades and Diplomas. Competition coaching where appropriate.

Special educational needs

Experience with autistic learners (esp. Asperger's). Calm, structured lessons; incremental goals; positive reinforcement.

Lesson format

50 minutes. Weekly sessions in term time. Studio in SW7; online available on request.

Rates: On enquiry.

Studio & Location

Lessons take place in a quiet studio at 11 Queen's Gate, South Kensington (SW7) on a Yamaha grand piano. Easy access from Gloucester Road and South Kensington stations.
